Urgent need right now

😷
Máscaras respiradoras — urgente
5+ days have passed since the earthquake. With so many unrecovered victims, the biological risk is critical: disease, odors and environmental contamination. COVID masks and basic surgical masks are NOT sufficient. Half-face or full-face respirators with combined filters are needed. Valid models:

Half mask: 3M 6200 · 3M 7502 · Moldex 7000 · Dräger X-plore 3300 · Honeywell North 7700
Full face: 3M 6800 · Moldex 9000 · Dräger X-plore 5500
Disposable with activated carbon: 3M 8214 · 3M 8514

Los filtros válidos son: 3M 60926, 3M 6006, 3M 2097, Moldex 7600, Dräger A1B1E1K1 Hg P3 o cartuchos combinados N-Series de Honeywell.

Coordinate shipment through ayudaencamino.com →
